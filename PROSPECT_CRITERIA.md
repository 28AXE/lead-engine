# Prospect Qualification Criteria

**Last Updated:** 2026-04-28
**Strategy:** Multi-vertical expansion beyond casinos

---

## Target Verticals

### 1. 🎰 Online Casinos (Core Vertical)
**Why:** High margins, urgent growth needs, crypto-native, performance marketing DNA

| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Domain Age | < 12 months (ideally < 3 months) |
| Budget Signal | 3+ influencers on Twitch/YouTube/IG/TikTok |
| Gap | 0-20 active Meta ads |
| Payment | Crypto-friendly (BTC/ETH/USDT) |
| License | Valid gambling license required |

**Hook Angle:** Competitor spy intel — "Your competitor X is running Y creative, getting Z impressions"

---

### 2. ☀️ Subsidized/High-Ticket Industries (Solar, Heat Pumps, EV Chargers)
**Why:** Government subsidies = customers don't pay full price, high lead value, can afford aggressive ad spend

**Sub-verticals:**
- Solar panel installation
- Heat pumps (pompes à chaleur)
- EV charging stations
- Home insulation (rénovation énergétique)
- Roofing (toiture)

| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Domain Age | < 2 years (more lenient — these businesses have cash) |
| Geo | FR/DE/UK/NL/ES (subsidy-rich markets) |
| Budget Signal | Running Google Ads OR local TV/radio OR radio |
| Gap | Weak or no Meta presence (check Ad Library) |
| Certification | RGE certified (FR), MCS (UK), etc. = legitimate |

**Hook Angle:** "Government subsidies are driving demand on Meta — your competitor X is capturing those leads, you're not"

**Why this vertical:**
- Subsidies = €10k-30k deals, customer pays €3k-5k out of pocket
- One close = €5k-15k profit for client
- They CAN afford €3k-10k/month ad spend
- Meta is UNDERUTILIZED vs Google Ads

---

### 3. 🛒 E-commerce Requiring Whitelisting (Restricted Categories)
**Why:** They NEED our whitelisting service = sticky relationship, can bundle with ads

**Sub-verticals:**
- CBD / Hemp products
- Vaping / E-cigarettes
- Supplements / Weight loss
- Adult products (lingerie, toys)
- Alcohol (direct-to-consumer)
- Crypto/Finance products
- Dating apps
- Gaming / Loot boxes

| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Domain Age | < 18 months |
| Budget Signal | $10k+/mo ad spend (estimate via Ad Library) |
| Gap | Running ads BUT getting rejected/limited frequently |
| Signal | "Ad account disabled" complaints on Reddit/Twitter |
| Revenue | €1M+ ARR (can afford whitelisting + management) |

**Hook Angle:** "I can get you whitelisted — here's a client we unblocked after Meta rejected them 3x"

---

### 4. 📱 Apps & SaaS (Performance-First)
**Why:** Understand ROAS, LTV, quick decisions, crypto-friendly mindset

**Sub-verticals:**
- Gaming apps (UA)
- Fintech apps
- Dating apps
- AI tools
- Betting apps (sportsbooks, fantasy)

| Criterion | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| Funding | Seed+ raised (check Crunchbase) |
| Budget Signal | Hiring for performance marketing roles |
| Gap | CAC too high on Google, want Meta diversification |
| Signal | Active on Reddit/TikTok but weak on Meta |

**Hook Angle:** "Your CAC on Google is €X — Meta can be 30-50% lower for your vertical. Here's the data."

## Universal Scoring System (All Verticals)

### Domain Age Score
| Age | Score | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| < 3 months | 10 | Urgent growth mode |
| 3-6 months | 9 | |
| 6-12 months | 7 | |
| 12-18 months | 5 | |
| 18-24 months | 3 | |
| 2-3 years | 2 | Only if high-budget vertical (solar, etc.) |
| > 3 years | 0-1 | Disqualify unless exceptional |

### Meta Ad Activity Score
| Active Ads | Score | Interpretation |
|------------|-------|----------------|
| 0 ads | 8 | Gap to exploit (or they're blocked) |
| 1-10 ads | 9 | Testing phase — opportunity |
| 11-30 ads | 6 | Some presence — can improve |
| 31-100 ads | 3 | Already invested — harder to displace |
| 100+ ads | 0 | They're committed — avoid |

### Budget Signal Score (Influencers, Google Ads, etc.)
| Signal | Score |
|--------|-------|
| 10+ influencers / heavy Google Ads | 9 |
| 5-9 influencers / moderate Google Ads | 8 |
| 3-4 influencers / some Google Ads | 7 |
| 1-2 influencers / minimal paid | 5 |
| 0 paid marketing presence | 0 (disqualify — no marketing DNA) |
